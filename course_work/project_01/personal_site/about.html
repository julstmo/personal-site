Exercising

Going to the gym and exercising/lifting weights is one of my favorite things to do. I go to the gym about 3 to 4 times a week and I find that it truly helps me in a variety of different ways. It's great not only for keeping me active and healthy physically, but it also helps to boost my overall mood and confidence. Crushing your fitness goals and meeting like-minded people is a plus too.

Writing

Writing is another passion of mine and I do it practically every day in one form or another. Whether it's jotting down notes on paper, typing them out on my laptop, or putting ideas into the various different clouds on my phone, writing is something that I really enjoy and helps to stimulate my mind and creativity. The types of writing I typically do are that of fictional stories.

Movies and Music

My two biggest inspirations for the writing that I do definitely come from the movies that I watch and the music that I listen to; both of which I could not live without. I love going out to the theater to watch a new horror film or discovering new songs on Bandcamp, Soundcloud, or YouTube as I write. Both movies and music keep me inspired and motivated in my everyday life.
