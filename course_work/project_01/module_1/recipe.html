Shrimp Stock

Great shrimp stock to use as a base for gumbo, etouffee, or any other seafood dish. Freeze in ice cube trays to thaw as needed for later use.

Ingredients

  • shrimp shells from 2 pounds of shrimp, or taste
  • 1/2 cup roughly chopped onion
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1/4 cup roughly chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup celery leaves, or to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 8 cups cold water

Directions

  1. Combine shrimp shells, onion, lemon, chopped celery, celery leaves, garlic, peppercorns, thyme, and bay leaves in a saucepan.
  2. Cover with water.
  3. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to low.
  4. Simmer until reduced by 1/2, skimming off foam as necessary, 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Strain and use or freeze for later use.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:

72 calories; protein 11.8g; carbohydrates 6g; fat 0.9g; cholesterol 89.6mg; sodium 148.8mg
